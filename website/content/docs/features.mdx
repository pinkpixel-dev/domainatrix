Domainatrix provides a comprehensive set of features for managing, monitoring, and diagnosing your domain names. Here is a detailed look at how to use these tools.

---

## 1. Dashboard & Analytics

The main dashboard is designed to give you a birds-eye view of your domain portfolio. It highlights key metrics and potential issues so you can take action before a domain or SSL certificate expires.

![Dashboard Overview](/screenshots/screenshot1.png)

### Expiry Timeline
At the center of the dashboard is the **12-Month Expiry Timeline**. This is a custom, interactive visual timeline that maps out all of your domains by their expiration dates over the coming year.
*   Domains expiring within **30 days** are highlighted in red.
*   Domains expiring within **30–60 days** are highlighted in orange.
*   Domains with longer lifetimes are shown in green or gray.

### Risk & Cost Cards
The top of the dashboard displays key summary metrics:
*   **Annual Cost**: The sum of all domain renewal costs for the year (helping you budget).
*   **Missing Expiry**: Alerting you to domains whose expiry dates are currently unknown or haven't been fetched yet.
*   **SSL Expiring**: The number of domains with SSL certificates expiring within 30 days.
*   **Auto-Renew Disabled**: A count of domains that will not automatically renew, which could lead to accidental expiration.
*   **Notification Coverage**: The percentage of domains that have active notifications configured.

## 2. Domain Inventory

The **Domains** page allows you to view, search, and manage your portfolio. You can toggle between a dense list view or a rich card grid.

![Domain Inventory](/screenshots/screenshot2.png)

### Search & Advanced Filtering
- **Fuzzy Search**: Instantly filter your domains by name or registrar.
- **Status Filters**: Filter domains by their health status (Active, Warn, Error, or Untracked).
- **Registrar Filter**: Quickly narrow down your view to domains registered with specific registrars (e.g., Namecheap, Cloudflare, GoDaddy).

### Category Tagging
Assign custom, color-coded tags to your domains to keep things organized. You can easily group domains by project, client, or purpose (e.g., `prod`, `dev`, `marketing`, `parked`).

## 3. One-Click Diagnostics & Auto-Enrichment

The core strength of Domainatrix is its automated enrichment engine. When you click **Enrich** on any domain, the application executes multiple background checks and populates your database with detailed diagnostics.

![Core Diagnostics](/screenshots/screenshot4.png)

### Diagnostic Fields Captured
*   **WHOIS Details**: Retrieves registrar, registration date, last update date, expiration date, registrant email, and official registry status codes.
*   **DNS Records**: Automatically queries and displays vital DNS records, including:
    *   **NS** (Name Servers)
    *   **MX** (Mail Exchangers)
    *   **TXT** (Text records, often used for verification)
    *   **A** & **AAAA** (IPv4 and IPv6 target addresses)
*   **SSL Certificate Status**: Connects directly to the domain's server over TLS to extract the SSL certificate issuer (e.g., Let's Encrypt), validation status, and remaining days of validity.
*   **Network Geolocation**: Resolves the target IP to find its physical location, including the country, city, ISP, and organization hosting the site.

## 4. Uptime Monitoring & Performance

Domainatrix tracks your websites' availability in real-time, allowing you to ensure they remain accessible to your users.

![Uptime & Expiry Rings](/screenshots/screenshot3.png)

### Uptime Activity Grid
On the domain details page, a 12-week GitHub-style grid shows the results of recent uptime checks.
*   Each block represents a check window.
*   Hovering over a block displays the exact timestamp and response latency in milliseconds.
*   The overall average uptime percentage is displayed in the header.

### Expiry Rings
Beside the activity grid, two circular progress rings visually track the remaining time before your domain registration and SSL certificate expire.

### Core Web Vitals (CrUX)
If a `CRUX_API_KEY` is configured, a performance panel will load. This retrieves real-world user metrics from the Google Chrome UX Report, visualizing:
*   **LCP (Largest Contentful Paint)**: Loading performance.
*   **CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift)**: Visual stability.
*   **INP (Interaction to Next Paint)**: Responsiveness.

## 5. Subdomain Discovery

Identify all active subdomains under a primary domain to verify DNS hygiene and find hidden services.

![Subdomain Discovery](/screenshots/screenshot5.png)

Domainatrix combines two powerful techniques for discovery:
1.  **Certificate Transparency Logs**: Queries the public `crt.sh` logs for certificates issued to subdomains.
2.  **DNS Wordlist Probing**: Concurrently checks a built-in list of common subdomain prefixes (e.g., `dev`, `staging`, `api`, `mail`) to see if they resolve to an active IP address.

Once discovered, all subdomains are listed with their status (Live or Unresolved), IPs, and the method by which they were found.

## 6. Notification Center & Logs

Stay updated on critical changes in your portfolio. All events are logged in the app and can be pushed to your active communication channels.

![Notification Center](/screenshots/screenshot6.png)

*   **Change Detection**: Whenever a domain is scanned (manually or by cron), Domainatrix compares new results against the database. If there is a change in the IP address, DNS records, registrar, or SSL configuration, an event is logged.
*   **Uptime Alerts**: If an uptime check fails (or recovers), an alert is generated.
*   **Expiry Reminders**: Generates alerts when domains or certificates are 30, 14, 7, and 1 days away from expiring.
*   **External Integration**: Delivers alerts to Discord, Slack, or Email (SMTP). If a network issue prevents delivery, you can retry from the Notification Center.
